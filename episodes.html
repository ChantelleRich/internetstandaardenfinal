Our Featured Episodes!

Episode 6: Anime Pet Peeves

"Anime fans won't like this!"

In this episode, we dive into our biggest anime pet peeves! From overused tropes to cringe-worthy character designs. Whether you're a seasoned otaku or a casual fan, join the conversation and laugh along. Get ready for some heated debates and relatable rants—because even the biggest anime fans have their limits!

Episode 5: Live-Action Anime

"Stop ruining our favorite animes"

In this episode, we discuss the world of live-action anime adaptations! From the hit or miss casting choices to the challenges of bringing animated worlds to life, we explore what works (and what doesn't) in these high-stakes adaptations. Join the conversation as we share our thoughts on live-action anime films and series, from Death Note to One Piece.

Episode 4: Anime Speed Dating

"Are 2D men better than real men?"

In this episode, we mix romance with geek culture as we dive into the world of speed dating... anime character style! Tune in as we each choose our dream anime dates, from charming heroes to mysterious villains, and imagine what a fast-paced, nerdy matchmaking session would look like. Who would win their hearts, and who would we swipe left on?!

Episode 3: Old Gen VS New Gen

"New Gen Anime is Nothing Compared to the Classics"

In this episode, we dive into the ultimate debate: old gen vs. new gen! We explore how the anime landscape has evolved, from classic series that defined the genre to the fresh hits shaping the current scene. Join the conversation as we compare animation styles, storytelling trends, and fan culture, discussing which era reigns supreme—and which has our hearts.
